!tell <nick> <message>
    Leave a message for nick. They will receive it the next
    time they join the channel. The nick parameter is not case
    sensitive.
        Examples of tell for a user with nick "AbcD123" with
        the message "hello world":
            !tell AbcD123 hello world
            !tell abcd123 hello world
            !tell ABCD123 hello world
            !tell aBCd123 hello world
